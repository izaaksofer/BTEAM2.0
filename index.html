The Course



Founders & Coders

Founders & Coders is a course run by Dan Sofer and Pastor Jurado. The aim is to take a small group of highly motivated students and provide them with a world class education, with as little cost as possible.

High Intensity

Students are expected to reach a level where they can begin work as a junior developer, or start work on their own project. Natural aptitude is welcome. Enthusiasm and hard work are a requirement.

Low Cost

The course is free. Dan's philosophy is to create value in his buisness by creating value in his students, whether they join him in other projects or help him get sponsorship from the industry.

Group Learning

Borrowing from the ideas of Richard Felda and Sugata Mitra, this course is built on a philosophy of group learning through ambitious, open ended, projects.
